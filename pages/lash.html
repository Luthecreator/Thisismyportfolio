Ad. for lash brand FabulitSA

FabulitSA is a business of mine that I started late 2020, during the Covid-19 lockdown period. I had gotten the idea from a business my aunt had run before and added my own twist to it by not just selling hair, but including lashes and then eventually makeup products. I didn't make a lot of sales during 2021,but I continued pushing, trying to find what I was trying to sell and what type of aestetic I was going for, to what type of audience I wanted to attract.

In this concept I focused my attention on bringing my lash products to life. So lighting, dominance, scale and contrast were important in bringing emphasis to the main focal points, which were the lashes.

I used a black and white filter because I thought it brought the concepts to life compared to normal colour. The shadows at the back were softened , the contrast of black and greys between the lash applicator stick and the lash for balance and emphasis of the lashes. I did not want lighting to be too exposed, I added a little bit of exposure to further seperate the foreground from the background.
